# Research Preflight Gate

Use this manual review after extraction and before running the generated gate.

## Completion

- Does the packet answer every central part of the user's actual assignment?
- Was every reasonably obtainable load-bearing source inspected during this task?
- Are remaining unknowns genuinely unavailable, future, or outside the agreed boundary?
- Does optional original reporting contain only interviews, FOIA/public-record requests, proprietary material, site visits, expert consultation, original analysis, or future evidence?
- Has every resolvable hostile-review criticism been converted into research and fixed?

## Source skepticism

- Was any claim accepted because a source was prestigious, peer reviewed, official, mainstream, dissident, or ideologically appealing?
- For each central source, is the audit limited to claims the source can actually establish?
- Do official statements prove only announcement or position unless implementation evidence was inspected?
- Were media reports followed to underlying documents, data, recordings, or named evidence where reasonably available?
- Were advocacy, industry, state, academic, and outsider sources subjected to the same claim-level tests?

## Method and data

- Are provenance, unit, denominator, measurement, classification, missingness, exclusions, assumptions, uncertainty, and reproducibility addressed where applicable?
- Were central studies and reports read beyond abstracts and summaries?
- Were material calculations or classifications reproduced or explicitly marked not reproducible with reasons?
- Does the public claim match the source's actual result, magnitude, population, time period, and uncertainty?
- For causal claims, are mechanism, counterfactual, alternatives, confounders, counterevidence, and revision conditions explicit?

## Contradiction and viewpoint review

- Was the strongest available contradiction searched for and represented?
- Are real viewpoints grounded in named people, institutions, records, texts, or movements?
- Were factual claims from every viewpoint checked rather than merely summarized?
- Were incentives and motive language handled symmetrically?
- Were relevant perspectives derived from the issue rather than filled from a fixed ideological list?

## Writer usefulness

- Can the packet stand alone without requiring the CSVs for basic comprehension?
- Does the Assignment And Boundary section state the reader baseline, assumed knowledge, and knowledge-transfer goal?
- Does `reader-education.csv` register every central question, mechanism, controversy, and material comparison case and map every load-bearing claim to at least one central module?
- Does every Education Brief module teach orientation, sequence or mechanism, named evidence, dispute or limit, and article relevance in connected prose before any table compresses it?
- Does every material case-comparison row carry exactly one matching registered `Module ID` in its designated column?
- Did a reader given only the packet reconstruct every central module and return an unqualified pass with no comprehension failure or required revision?
- Does it explain the system, history, technical machinery, and causal reasoning needed to write with authority?
- Are article directions clearly separated into ready, promising, and unsupported?
- Are tempting claims to avoid paired with the evidence that would be required?
- Does the completion statement say `No further Codex research pass is required for the stated boundary.`?

If a fix is possible with available tools, continue researching. If the record is genuinely unavailable, finish with an evidence-limited verdict rather than suggesting another Codex pass.
